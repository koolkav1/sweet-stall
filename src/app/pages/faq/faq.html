Finer Details

Common questions regarding our service, logistics, and bespoke offerings.

How far in advance should we book? expand_more

We recommend booking 6-9 months in advance for peak wedding season (May to September). For off-peak dates, 3-6 months is usually sufficient. However, we occasionally have last-minute availability, so please don't hesitate to inquire.

Do you cater to specific dietary requirements? expand_more

Absolutely. We work closely with our artisan suppliers to accommodate vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, and other dietary needs. Simply let us know during your consultation, and we'll curate a menu that delights every guest.

What is included in the standard hire? expand_more

Standard hire includes the styled cart, a curated selection of artisan confections, elegant glassware and servingware, signage, full setup and takedown, and a dedicated host on site to replenish and style throughout the event.

Can we customize the cart decor to match our theme? expand_more

Yes, bespoke styling is at the heart of what we do. We'll work with your color palette, floral choices, and overall aesthetic to design a cart that feels like a natural extension of your celebration.

How long is the hire period? expand_more

Our standard hire covers up to five hours of service at your venue, with setup completed before guest arrival. Extended hire can be arranged on request — just let us know your timeline when you enquire.
